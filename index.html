What is DiffChain?

DiffChain is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital block chain that enables you to build decentralized applications in Python, with high scalability, speed and very, very low fees. Think of it as Ethereum, but built DIFFerent.

The fun and friendly internet currency?

DiffChains sets itself apart from other digital currencies by making it EASY to make decentralized apps in Python. Not some crazy special programming language that then costs 5,000$ to launch after you make it :( . Also there is an amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you."
